My Major

As stated earlier, I am a Commercial Aviation major here at UND. My ultimate career goal is to work as a demonstration pilot for an aircraft sales firm, but before that, I plan to be a Certified Flight Instructor (CFI) and Regional Airline Pilot. Currently, I am working on my CFI certificate, and I am about two weeks away from taking the test to recieve that certificate. It has been a tough flight course, but I have learned about some pretty important things. I've learned effective communication techniques, teaching methods, as well as fine tuned my flying skills as a commercial pilot. I took my commercial checkride this summer and it was an exciting experience.

Here I am with the Piper Seminole, the multi engine training aircraft that I am qualified to fly.

In addition to flying within the College of Aerospace, I also work for the College of Aerospace. I am a Student Ambassador and Tour Guide. As a Student Ambassador, I run the UND Aerospace social media, write for their alumni magazine as a student contributer, and lead current and prospective student informative panels. I answer the phones and student emails in the UND Aerospace information call center as well. As a student Tour Guide, I give tours to current and prospective students, as well as train other tour guides.

The job is rewarding and fun, and has provided me with some great experiences and connections. Here I am at work.
